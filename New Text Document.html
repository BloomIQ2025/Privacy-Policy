We share your information only with certified life coaches who are actively seeking new clients. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other third parties.

By submitting your information, you consent to be contacted by life coaches regarding coaching services. You may request to opt out of future contact or have your data deleted at any time by emailing us at lantern.leads@yahoo.com.

You have the right to:

To exercise these rights, please contact us at lantern.leads@yahoo.com.

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information, including secure storage and limited access. Data is stored in encrypted formats and accessed only by authorized personnel.
